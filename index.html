My Personal Interests

Acoustic Guitars

I taught myself the acoustic guitar in high school, which naturally led to countless hours of playing with other musicians for friends and family. I absolutely love serving as a guitar tutor for Seven Mile since 2019 as I am able to share my passion for music with talented young students in Detroit.

Community Involvement

In addition to tutoring guitar, I also contribute to Seven Mile as the Director of Music by assisting with our administration initiatives with local communities. This largely entails coordinating tutor and student attendance at multiple locations, scheduling public musical showcases at the end of each term, and overseeing financial spending.
